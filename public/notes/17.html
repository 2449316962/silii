OpenAI settings

The files with the settings are here (TavernAI\public\OpenAI Settings) You also can add own settings files.

Temperature

Value from 0 to 2.0. Lower value - the answers are more logical, but less creative. Higher value - the answers are more creative, but less logical.

Frequency Penalty

Frequency Penalty is responsible for the penalty of repeated words. If the character is fixated on something or repeats the same phrase, then increasing this parameter will fix it.

Presence penalty

Presence penalty lowers the probability of a word if it already appeared in the predicted text. you can think of Frequency Penalty as a way to prevent word repetitions, and Presence Penalty as a way to prevent topic repetitions.
