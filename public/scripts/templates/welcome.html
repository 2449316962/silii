What is Dechat?

  • Dechat is a private space for private conversations, using the latest available & uncensored AI models.
  • We have a wide range of available plugins and API extensions, making use of difussion models as well as trasformers. For those who want pictures, Text to Speech, etc.
  • Dechat's purpose is to bring human-machine conversations to an easy-to-modify space, granting insights, knowledge, and even spare time of the real world.

How to start chatting?

  1. Click and select a Chat API.
  2. Click and pick a character
